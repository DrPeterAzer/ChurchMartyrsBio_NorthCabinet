القديس يوحنا الحبيب

Saint John the Beloved

(١٣ يناير / ٤ طوبة) — (13 Jan / 4 Tuba)

He is the son of Zebedee and the brother of the Apostle James the Greater... He is the disciple whom Jesus loved (John 19:26). He is the one who leaned on Jesus’ chest during the Last Supper. He is the apostle who embodied the love of virginity and true greatness, heartfelt simplicity, and an extraordinary love...

He was the one who helped Peter enter the courtyard where Jesus was judged... He was the only disciple who stayed with the Lord until the crucifixion and to whom Jesus entrusted His mother Mary...

هو ابن زبدي، وشقيق الرسول يعقوب الكبير... هو التلميذ الذي كان يسوع يحبه (يو 19: 26). وهو الذي اتكأ على صدره في العشاء الأخير...

كان هو واسطة إدخال بطرس حيث حكم الرب يسوع نظرا لأنه كان معروفا عند رئيس الكهنة (يو 18: 15-16)...
